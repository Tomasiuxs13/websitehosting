Introduction & Overview

Squarespace has built its reputation as the website builder for creatives and design-conscious users. Founded in 2003 by Anthony Casalena in his dorm room, Squarespace has grown into a publicly traded company known for award-winning templates and sleek, minimalist designs.

What sets Squarespace apart is their uncompromising focus on aesthetics. Every template is designed by professionals and looks stunning out of the box. While this means less design freedom than Wix, it also means it's harder to create an ugly website on Squarespace.

Squarespace is particularly popular among photographers, artists, designers, and businesses that prioritize visual presentation. Their built-in analytics, e-commerce features, and marketing tools make it a complete solution for creative professionals.
