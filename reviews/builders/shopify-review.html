Introduction & Overview

Shopify is the undisputed leader in e-commerce platforms, powering over 4 million online stores worldwide. Founded in 2006 in Ottawa, Canada, Shopify has grown from a snowboard equipment store's internal solution to a publicly traded company worth billions.

What makes Shopify special is its laser focus on selling. While other website builders add e-commerce as a feature, Shopify was built from the ground up for online retail. This shows in everything from their inventory management to their point-of-sale systems.

Shopify serves businesses of all sizes, from solo entrepreneurs selling handmade crafts to enterprise brands processing millions in sales. Their ecosystem of apps, themes, and integrations makes it possible to build almost any type of online store.
