Introduction & Overview

Hostinger Website Builder is included free with Hostinger's web hosting plans, making it one of the best value propositions in the website builder market. Rather than paying separately for a builder and hosting, you get both in one affordable package.

The builder features AI-powered website generation that can create a complete website in minutes. Simply answer a few questions about your business, and the AI generates a personalized website with relevant content and images.

While not as feature-rich as standalone builders like Wix, Hostinger's builder offers excellent value for users who need a simple, professional website without breaking the bank. The combination of fast hosting and an intuitive builder makes it particularly attractive for small businesses and personal sites.
