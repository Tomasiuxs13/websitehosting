Introduction & Overview

Wix has established itself as the world's most popular website builder, powering over 250 million websites worldwide. Founded in 2006 in Israel, Wix has evolved from a simple Flash-based builder to a sophisticated platform with AI-powered design tools, e-commerce capabilities, and a vast ecosystem of apps and integrations.

What sets Wix apart is their commitment to making web design accessible to everyone. Their drag-and-drop editor requires zero coding knowledge, yet offers enough flexibility to create truly unique websites. The addition of Wix ADI (Artificial Design Intelligence) takes this further by generating complete websites based on your answers to a few questions.

In this comprehensive review, we'll examine whether Wix lives up to its reputation as the best website builder in 2025. We've built multiple sites on the platform, tested their features extensively, and compared them to leading alternatives.

Features Deep Dive

Wix Editor

The Wix Editor is the heart of the platform. Unlike grid-based builders, Wix offers true pixel-perfect control – you can place any element anywhere on the page. This freedom allows for creative designs but requires more attention to ensure mobile responsiveness.

Wix ADI (AI Builder)

Wix ADI creates a complete website for you based on a few questions about your business. It selects images, writes content, and designs layouts automatically. While the results are impressive for quick starts, most users will want to customize further.

Templates & Design

With over 800 designer-made templates across every industry, Wix offers the most extensive template library available. Templates are fully customizable, and you can change colors, fonts, and layouts to match your brand perfectly.

E-commerce (Wix Stores)

Wix Stores provides comprehensive e-commerce functionality including product management, inventory tracking, multiple payment gateways, shipping integrations, and abandoned cart recovery. It's suitable for small to medium online stores.

Wix App Market

The App Market offers 300+ apps to extend your site's functionality – from booking systems and email marketing to live chat and social media integration. Many apps are free, while others require subscriptions.

Ease of Use

Wix excels at making website building accessible to complete beginners. The interface is intuitive, with helpful tooltips and tutorials throughout. Most users can create a basic website within an hour of signing up.

The drag-and-drop editor works exactly as you'd expect – click on any element and drag it where you want. The freedom can be overwhelming at first, but Wix provides guidelines and snap-to-grid features to help maintain alignment.

Ease of Use Rating: 9.5/10

Wix is one of the easiest website builders to use, with intuitive controls and helpful guidance throughout the design process.
