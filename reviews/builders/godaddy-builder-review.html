Introduction & Overview

GoDaddy Websites + Marketing is GoDaddy's proprietary website builder, designed for small business owners who want to get online quickly. The AI-powered builder can create a complete website in minutes based on your business type and preferences.

Unlike traditional website builders that give you a blank canvas, GoDaddy's approach is to generate a ready-to-use website that you can then customize. This makes it ideal for busy business owners who don't have time to design from scratch.

The "Marketing" part of the name isn't just branding – GoDaddy includes email marketing, social media tools, and SEO features in their plans, making it a comprehensive small business solution.
