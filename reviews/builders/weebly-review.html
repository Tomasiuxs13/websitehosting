Introduction & Overview

Weebly is one of the original website builders, founded in 2006 and acquired by Square in 2018. This acquisition brought powerful e-commerce and point-of-sale capabilities to the platform, making it particularly attractive for small businesses that sell both online and in-person.

Weebly takes a simpler approach than competitors like Wix. While this means fewer customization options, it also means a gentler learning curve. If you want to build a basic website quickly without getting overwhelmed by options, Weebly delivers.

The Square integration is Weebly's secret weapon. If you use Square for payment processing or point-of-sale, Weebly provides seamless integration that competitors can't match.

Final Verdict: 8.8/10

Weebly is a solid choice for beginners and Square users who want a simple, affordable website. While it lacks the features of competitors, its ease of use is unmatched.
