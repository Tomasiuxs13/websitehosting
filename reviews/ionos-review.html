Introduction & Company Background

IONOS (formerly 1&1 IONOS) is one of Europe's largest web hosting providers and a major player in the global hosting market. Founded in 1988 in Germany as 1&1 Internet AG, the company has grown to serve over 8 million customers worldwide with a comprehensive range of web services.

What distinguishes IONOS from competitors is their personal consultant service – every customer gets assigned a dedicated advisor who provides personalized support. This human touch is increasingly rare in an industry dominated by chatbots and ticket systems.

IONOS is part of United Internet AG, a publicly traded German company. They operate data centers across Europe and North America, providing robust infrastructure for businesses of all sizes. Their European heritage means strong GDPR compliance and data protection standards.

In this review, we'll examine IONOS's hosting services, their unique personal consultant feature, and whether their rock-bottom introductory pricing delivers real value.

Features Deep Dive

Personal Consultant Service

IONOS's standout feature is their personal consultant service. Every customer is assigned a dedicated advisor who can help with account setup, technical questions, and ongoing support. This personalized approach is unique in the budget hosting space.

Hosting Options

IONOS offers shared hosting, WordPress hosting, VPS, dedicated servers, and cloud hosting. Their shared hosting includes SSD storage, free SSL, and email accounts. WordPress hosting comes with automatic updates and staging environments.

Website Builder

IONOS includes their MyWebsite builder with hosting plans. The drag-and-drop builder is beginner-friendly and includes industry-specific templates, though it's not as flexible as dedicated builders like Wix.

Security Features

All plans include free SSL certificates, DDoS protection, and daily backups. Their SiteLock security scanning is available as an add-on for malware detection and removal.

Support Rating: 8.5/10
