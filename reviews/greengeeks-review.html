Introduction & Company Background

GreenGeeks has carved out a unique niche in the web hosting industry as the leading eco-friendly hosting provider. Founded in 2008 by Trey Gardner, the company has built its brand around environmental responsibility, claiming to put 3x the energy they consume back into the grid through renewable energy credits.

What makes GreenGeeks interesting is that they've proven eco-friendly hosting doesn't mean sacrificing performance. They use modern infrastructure including LiteSpeed web servers, SSD storage, and a built-in CDN – the same technologies used by leading traditional hosts.

GreenGeeks is EPA Green Power Partner certified and has been recognized for their environmental commitments. For website owners who care about their carbon footprint, GreenGeeks offers a way to host responsibly without compromising on features.

In this review, we'll examine whether GreenGeeks's environmental focus affects their hosting quality, and whether they can compete with traditional providers on performance and value.

Features Deep Dive

Environmental Commitment

GreenGeeks purchases 3x the energy they consume in the form of wind energy credits. This means for every unit of energy used, they put three units of renewable energy back into the grid. They're EPA Green Power Partner certified and have offset over 615,000 tons of carbon dioxide.

Performance Technology

Despite their green focus, GreenGeeks doesn't skimp on performance. They use LiteSpeed web servers with LSCache, SSD storage in RAID-10 configuration, and include Cloudflare CDN on all plans. PHP 8.x support ensures compatibility with modern applications.

WordPress Features

GreenGeeks offers managed WordPress hosting with automatic updates, one-click staging, and WordPress-specific caching. Their platform is optimized for WordPress performance with pre-installed caching plugins.

Security Features

All plans include free SSL certificates, nightly backups with easy restore, real-time security scanning, and proactive server monitoring. Their security measures are comprehensive for shared hosting.
