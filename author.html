Who I Am

I’m the creator of PickWebsiteHosting, a project that started as my own notebook of hosting tests and grew into a full resource for anyone trying to pick the right web host. I’ve run blogs, small business sites, and e‑commerce projects, and I’ve felt the pain of slow, unreliable hosting more than once.

Today, my goal is simple: cut through the marketing claims and give you clear, data‑driven recommendations that are actually useful in the real world.

How I Test Hosting

Real Accounts, No Shortcuts

I sign up with real paid accounts, set up test sites, and monitor uptime, speed, and reliability over time – just like a normal customer would experience.

Performance Benchmarks

I track metrics like Time to First Byte (TTFB), full load time, and uptime percentages across multiple locations to see how each host behaves under realistic conditions.

Support Stress‑Tests

I contact support with real questions, measure response times, and rate how helpful and accurate the answers are across chat and other channels.

Fine‑Print & Pricing

I read the terms, renewal pricing, and add‑ons carefully so you’re not surprised by hidden fees, upsells, or aggressive renewal jumps later.

Editorial Values

  • Transparency: If a host is an affiliate partner, I still highlight the downsides clearly.
  • Practical Advice: Every recommendation is tied to real‑world use cases, not just “top lists”.
  • Beginner‑Friendly: I explain jargon in plain English so you can make decisions without being a sysadmin.
